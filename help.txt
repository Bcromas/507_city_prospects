
***Help/Command Menu***

'Add city': Crawl & scrape Zillow.com for apartments in a given city. NOTE: can take several minutes.

'Visuals': View data on a single city or compare two cities. Options include: 
1 - scatter plot of rent & square feet for a city
2 - box plots of rent & square feet for a city
3 - stacked bars comparing rent in 2 cities
4 - table of averages comparing 2 cities.

'Delete_db': Delete & rebuild the DB.

'Exit': Return to previous screen or exit program if at main menu.
